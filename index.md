## 🧭 About Me

Hey there! I’m Aryan Mathur, a final-year undergraduate at the Indian Institute of Technology Palakkad, majoring in Electrical Engineering.
My work bridges the fields of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Robotics — driven by the goal of building intelligent, scalable, and interpretable systems that can perceive and reason about the world.

I’ve interned at Accenture (Advanced Application Engineering) and EasyAlgo Research, contributed to AI competitions like the Amazon ML Challenge and Adobe Research Challenges, and authored an arXiv publication on Deep Learning for Video Understanding (2024).

Beyond the technical front, I’ve led the Inter IIT Tech Meet contingent, founded IIT Palakkad’s Entrepreneurship Cell, and organized developer communities as a Google Developers Group Lead. These experiences shaped my ability to combine leadership with innovation, and research with collaboration.

I’m now focused on exploring graduate-level research in machine learning, reinforcement learning, and multimodal perception, particularly in the context of intelligent systems and robotic reasoning.

> “Building intelligent systems that bridge AI, engineering, and design.”
